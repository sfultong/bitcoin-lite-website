Bitcoin Unified

Bitcoin Unified is a fork of the litecoin codebase that starts a new blockchain with balances taken from a snapshot of the bitcoin ledger. The idea is to mimic the economic characteristics of bitcoin, while allowing for higher transactions-per-second.

Bitcoin Maximalism

The bitcoin-lite developers believe strongly that the majority of existing cryptocurrency investment should be preserved in every new blockchain, so as to avoid needlessly competing interests within the community as much as possible.

Bitcoin-Spinoff-Toolkit

Bitcoin Unified utilizes the bitcoin-spinoff-toolkit to allow bitcoin holders to claim their share of bitcoin-lite coins. The bitcoin-spinoff-toolkit is eventually intended to make it easy to start a new blockchain with bitcoin balances using the code of any new cryptocurrency technology.
