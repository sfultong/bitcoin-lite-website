Bitcoin Unified

Bitcoin Unified is a fork of the litecoin codebase that starts a new blockchain with balances taken from snapshots of the bitcoin, litecoin and dogecoin ledgers. The idea is to preserve the wealth of existing cryptocurrency holders in a techology that has superior scalability to bitcoin.

Unification

The bitcoin-lite developers believe strongly that the majority of existing cryptocurrency investment should be preserved in every new blockchain, so as to avoid needlessly competing interests within the community as much as possible.
